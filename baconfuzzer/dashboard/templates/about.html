About Bacon Fuzzer

The Bacon Fuzzer is intended to be a light-weight, open-source tool to enable rapid specification and deployment of a fuzzer for specific message formats and data structures. It is not a feature-rich or high fidelity mechanism, and is targetted at running against networked or physically local devices or software.
